International Conference on Computational Intelligence in Data Mining (ICCIDM-2016) provides an international forum for presentation of original research findings, as well as exchange and dissemination of innovative, practical development experiences in different fields of engineering. The conference draws researchers and application developers from a wide range of data mining and computational Intelligence related areas along with their algorithms and applications of current issues of almost all branches of Engineering and Technology. After the two successful versions of ICCIDM, this conference has tried grounding every aspect related to Data Mining, covering all the possible research areas and crux. Awareness of Data Mining and its application is becoming popular among the general population. Parallel offers of hope add woes to the researchers of Data Mining due to the potential limitations experienced in the real-time.

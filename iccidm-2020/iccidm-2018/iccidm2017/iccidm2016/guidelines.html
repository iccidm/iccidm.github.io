AUTHOR GUIDELINES

Submission Details

The Original unpublished Research Papers, Articles & Working papers having maximum length 12 pages on the topics related to the theme are invited for presentation/publication in the conference proceedings.

(1) Kindly ensure that your paper is formatted as per Springer AISC Template (not exceeding 8 pages written in A4 size). Extra pages (beyond 8 maximum 12 pages) are liable to extra page payment charges. Please refer the attached springer template for preparation of your paper.

(2) All papers must be submitted online via easychair submission portal using the following link: link:https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=iccidm2016

(3) All submissions will be thoroughly peer-reviewed by experts based on originality, significance and clarity.

(4) Only papers presenting original content with novel research results or successful innovative applications will be considered for publication in the conference proceedings (as chapters in edited volumes of Springer AISC Series).

.

Step A : Manuscript preparation:

a. The manuscript should be prepared in Springer LNCS format using MS word (.docx) . The guidelines given here may help you to prepare the manuscript.

b. The figures in the manuscript should be of good resolution (600 dpi or more), tables should not be in pictorial format. Figures should be visible within boundary of the text area of the page and MUST not go beyond it.

c. Do NOT use any Social / Academic titles (e.g. Mr.,Ms.,Dr.,Prof. etc) preceding the author names. Do NOT mention the position of a person (e.g., research scholar, student, assistant professor, professor, IEEE member, IEEE student member etc.).

d. Mention full address, affiliation, and email of ALL authors, specify a corresponding author with the corresponding e-mail ID. [Once an article is accepted, the publisher will send the proofreading of article to this e-mail].

e. Do not make page numbering and do not give any running head title.

f. Every article must bear a self-composed independent single paragraph as Abstract, which MUST NOT bear any citation or reference. Abstract MUST not have a section number.

g. Articles must be in spell checked and grammatically correct English.

h. All references, figures, and tables should be numbered in sequence starting from 1 and MUST be duly cited / referred within the text.

i. Deeply verify the referencing style of LNCS, which will help to produce correct reference format. A reference should bear following parameters in proper format: author name(s), title of article/book, conference/journal name, volume, issue, page numbers, and publication year.

j. All the manuscripts must be prepared using strict LNCS format of Springer.

Step B : After acceptance:

Registration includes ALL the following events to be completed by author within aforementioned deadline:

a. Preparing and uploading of the Camera Ready Article:

The final camera-ready copy of the papers must be in Springer LNCS format with a length of maximum 12 pages. Over-length charges will apply for each extra page beyond 8 pages.

However, it is desired that maximum number of pages in an article should not exceed 12 pages in any circumstance. Check Author Guidelines for formatting the camera ready article.

b. Paying Registration Fee:

Calculate the registration fee according to your page length and type of authorship as directed in REGISTRATION page and make payment using Foreign Telegraphic Transfer or Direct Transfer or NEFT Transfer or Wire transfer (preferable : Mention paper id and Author name in the remarks column during online transfer) or demand draft. Do not forget to keep the pay-in-slip or Challan or online payment receipt. You need the scanned copy of it to complete next steps.

c. Filling up of the Registration Form, which can be found in PDF format .

Step C : Preparing the presentation : :

Electronic presentation facility will be available and authors should prepare their presentations having maximum 15 slides. Presentation time is limited to 10-15 minutes including the question and answer session. The presentation should be crisp to clearly bring out the objective of the research and its outcomes along with its importance and applications, if any.

Recommendation for extending works for special issues will be on the basis of the value of the work as well as the presentation.

Note 1:

1.Voice over PPT is expected from participants who are unable to physically present their work at the conference.The mail MUST bear a subject line "Presentation slide of article ICCIDM Unique paper ID".

2.Papers ( with voice over PPT slides ) after the registration will only to be considered for the inclusion of the paper in the Springer proceedings but they will not to be considered for publication or extension of work in any of the special issue of ICCIDM-2016 journals.

3.Recommendation for extending works for journals will be on the basis of the value of the work as well as the presentation in ICCIDM-2016.

Note 2:

The over length charges beyond 8 pages (in LNCS format) will be added to the normal registration fees to calculate the actual registration fee to be paid by the authors.The over length charge is Rs.800 (for Indian authors) / USD 15( for foreign authors) per extra page for a maximum of 4 extra pages.

Note 3:

It is presumed that your paper is not submitted in any other conference/journals/book/book chapter and thus it is original. In any case, if your paper is found to have similarity over 30% with any other online submission elsewhere, the paper will not be published in the Springer proceedings.
